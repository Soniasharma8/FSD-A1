Pink Cherry Cake Recipe

A delicious and moist pink cherry cake that melts in your mouth!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup cherry puree
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • Pink food coloring (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a round cake pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, beat butter and sugar until creamy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Mix in the cherry puree and vanilla extract.
  5. In a separate bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with milk.
  7. If desired, add a drop of pink food coloring for a more vibrant cake.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  9. Let the cake cool before frosting with cherry buttercream.
